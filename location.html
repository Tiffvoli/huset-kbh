Come and visit us

At Husets biograf we’ll go the extra mile to create unique and fun movie experiences. In the old theater we show everything from cult classics to more indie, underground movies in all genres, you name it!

We also throw popular, interactive movie showings like Sing-along, where you can sing and dance along to Grease. If you’re not that into singing you can also join The Dudes and toast-along with cold white russians for the Movie Drink-Along where we show the movie classic The Big Labowski.

There is of course also room for the more traditional cinema experience with popcorn and soda, as we show boom-hit movies like The Avengers along with many others.
